What We Do

Jet Boat Services

The same level of care we give PWCs — scaled up for boats. Mobile to your dock or marina, or bring it to our Seaford shop.

🔧

Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Manufacturer-grade computer diagnostics, compression tests, oil analysis, cooling system inspection. Twin-engine setups welcome.

⚙️

Jet Pump & Drive Service

Impeller and wear ring replacement, drive shaft inspection, intake grate repair. The most common — and most overlooked — jet boat issue.

❄️

Winterization & Shrink Wrap

Proper Long Island winterization including engine fogging, antifreeze, battery storage, and pro shrink wrap to protect against snow and salt air.

🛟

Hull & Gel Coat Repair

Fiberglass and gel coat repair for dock dings, stress cracks, scratches, and structural damage. Matched finish so it looks factory.

🔋

Electrical & Electronics

Battery and charging system, MFD/Garmin/Lowrance display work, NMEA wiring, lighting, stereo systems, accessory installs.

🚚

Mobile Service

Don't want to trailer it? We come to your dock, marina slip, or driveway with a fully stocked van. Most service calls done on site.

📋

Insurance Claims

Storm damage, collision, theft — we document, estimate, and work with your insurer so you get a fair repair without the runaround.

🔍

Pre-Purchase Inspections

Buying a used jet boat? We'll inspect engine, jet drive, hull, and electronics before you commit. Learn more →
